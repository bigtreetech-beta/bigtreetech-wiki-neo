## Product Overview

The **BIQU CryoGrip Pro Series** represents the next generation of spring steel build plates. It is engineered to eliminate common 3D printing failures, including poor first-layer adhesion, high energy consumption, and accelerated wear of the heated bed. By providing exceptional grip at significantly lower temperatures, the CryoGrip Pro ensures stable and reliable prints with minimal bed heating. This innovative technology leads to higher print success rates and a notable reduction in power usage.

The CryoGrip Pro Series is available in two distinct product lines to suit different printing needs: **Frostbite Series** and **Glacier Series**.

## CryoGrip Pro Glacier Series

The **CryoGrip Pro Glacier** features a refined surface texture designed for effortless, beginner-friendly printing. Most common filaments print cleanly without the need for glue. PLA adheres securely at just **45°C**, while PETG requires only **60°C**—delivering reliable performance with significantly less heat. Lower operating temperatures dramatically reduce warping and "spaghetti" failures, especially on large-format prints. The advanced coating also improves energy efficiency, reduces nozzle clogging risk, and promotes more sustainable, stress-free printing.

- **Energy Efficient & Cost Saving**: Achieve ultra-strong adhesion at lower bed temperatures, significantly reducing power consumption and cutting electricity costs—without compromising print stability.

    <ImageView src={require('./img/g1.webp').default} width="30%"/>

- **Wide Material Compatibility**: Print with confidence across a broad range of materials, including PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, PA, PC, Nylon, glass fiber composites, and most engineering-grade filaments.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/accessories-docs/build-plate/cyrogrippro/img/g2.webp').default} width="30%"/>

- **Reliable, Ultra-strong Adhesion**: When properly cleaned, the surface provides superior grip compared to traditional PEI spring steel plates, dramatically reducing the risk of print detachment—even on large-format prints.

    <ImageView src={require('./img/g3.webp').default} width="30%"/>

- **Enhanced Bottom Surface Finish**: Delivers a smoother, more refined bottom layer than traditional PEI plates, helping your prints blend seamlessly with other surfaces while achieving a cleaner, more professional look.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/accessories-docs/build-plate/cyrogrippro/img/g4.webp').default} width="30%"/>

## CryoGrip Pro Frostbite Cool Plate

The **CryoGrip Pro Frostbite** features a more aggressive textured surface than Glacier, purpose-built for ultra-low-temperature performance. PLA adheres securely at just **30°C**, and PETG at only **50°C**, delivering ultra-strong adhesion with minimal heat. ABS should be printed at standard recommended temperatures. Frostbite is optimized specifically for PLA, PETG, and ABS and is not intended for other materials.

- **Ultra-Low Energy Consumption**: Achieves ultra-strong adhesion at exceptionally low bed temperatures, dramatically reducing power usage without sacrificing stability.

    <ImageView src={require('./img/f1.webp').default} width="30%"/>

- **Ultra-Strong Adhesion**: Even at 30°C, parts maintain stable adhesion, reducing detachment risk and improving success rates during large-format printing.

    <ImageView src={require('./img/f4.webp').default} width="30%"/>

- **Precision-engineered for PLA, PETG, and ABS**: Built to satisfy the essential needs of most users—so you don't pay extra for performance you don't need.

## QR Code Recognition (Bambu Lab Models)

Currently, only X1C, P2S, and H2 series printers support QR code detection during the printing process. If detection issues occur, you may disable Build Plate Detection feature in the printer settings to proceed with the print.

You may submit a request through the community forum. Feedback is reviewed regularly to prioritize and accelerate compatibility development for additional models.

- **X1C**:

    - Current Glacier plates include a QR code sticker compatible with the X1C to ensure proper recognition.

    - Current Frostbite plates have been adapted to the Bambu Lab Low Temperature Plate QR code; with the correct settings, you won't experience recognition problems.

- **P2S**: Glacier and Frostbite plates do not currently support automatic detection mode. Compatibility updates are under development. Users may also print community-developed adapter models if needed.

- **H2 series**: Glacier and Frostbite plates do not currently support automatic detection mode. Compatibility updates are under development. Users may also print community-developed adapter models if needed.

### Print Quality Optimization

CryoGrip Pro is engineered for stable, energy-efficient printing. If performance does not meet expectations, review the following:

- **Clean the Build Surface**: Surface contaminants such as dust, glue residue, or skin oils can reduce adhesion. For Frostbite - Clean with soapy water. For Glacier - Clean with alcohol or soapy water.

- **Verify Bed Temperature & Material Settings**: Confirm that slicer settings match the recommended bed temperature for your filament. Incorrect temperature settings may lead to adhesion failure.

- **Fine-Tune Based on Material**: Different filaments have unique characteristics; incorrect cooling or chamber temperatures can affect CryoGrip adhesion:

    - **For PLA**: If adhesion issues occur: Lower the chamber temperature; reduce auxiliary cooling fan speed during high-speed printing.

    - **For PETG**: If adhesion issues occur: Maintain a stable chamber temperature; reduce print speed and test again.

- **Optimize First Layer Settings**: While most printers use default first-layer settings, you can manually fine-tune these in your slicer for better adhesion. For example: adjust first-layer height, line width, bed/nozzle temperatures, and print speeds. These settings provide a solid foundation for the rest of the print and increase the overall success rate.

- **Print Difficult to Remove(Solutions)**:

    - Allow the plate to cool completely to room temperature.

    - Gently flex the plate to release the model. Use a scraper carefully if necessary.

     - Slightly reduce bed temperature in future prints if adhesion is excessive.

- **Surface Scratches from Scraper Use**: To enable glue-free, low-temperature printing, the coating is softer than textured PEI and may be more susceptible to scratching. Minor surface scratches from normal use will not significantly affect adhesion performance.

- **Residue or Marks on the Plate**: Most residue can be removed with alcohol or soapy water. Minor discoloration or first-layer imprints that cannot be fully removed do not affect long-term performance.
