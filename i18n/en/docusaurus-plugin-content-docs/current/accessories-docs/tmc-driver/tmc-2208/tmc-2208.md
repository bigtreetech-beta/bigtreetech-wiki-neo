## Product Overview

The TMC2208 is an ultra-quiet motor driver IC designed for two-phase stepper motors. Its pinout is compatible with many traditional drivers. TRINAMIC’s advanced stealthChop2 chopping technology ensures noise-free operation, maximum efficiency, and optimal motor torque. Its fast current regulation, combined with optional spreadCycle configurations, enables highly dynamic motion control. Integrated power MOSFETs can handle motor currents of up to 1.4 A RMS. Protection and diagnostic features ensure robust and reliable operation. A simple UART interface provides additional tuning and control options, and application-specific tuning parameters can be stored in OTP memory.
