# Panda Vent Installation Guide

## Preparation Before Installation

:::tip

Download the appropriate housing files for your printer model and print the housing according to the file specifications.

:::

:::info[Model Download]

Github Repo [bigtreetech/Panda-Vent](https://github.com/bigtreetech/Panda-Vent)

- [Panda Vent Smart Riser for P1 X1](https://github.com/bigtreetech/Panda-Vent/tree/master/3D%20Model/Mounting%20solution/P1X1)

- [Panda Vent Smart Riser for P2 X2](https://github.com/bigtreetech/Panda-Vent/tree/master/3D%20Model/Mounting%20solution/P2X2)

:::

### Required Tools and Materials

| Item | Specification / Quantity | Description |
|------|---------------------------|-------------|
| Self-tapping screws | `CM3*6`, 35 pcs | Used to secure the printed parts, the PCB, and decorative covers |
| Hex Key | `2.0 mm` | User-provided |

:::warning

Before starting the installation, verify that all printed parts are oriented correctly and that the interfaces and screw holes are properly aligned. Do not force parts together, as this may damage the printed components.

:::

### Assemble Printed Parts GH

### Assemble the Front Decorative Printed Parts

- Install the left and right front decorative printed parts onto the corresponding positions on both sides of the front frame.

- Combine `Printed Part F`, `Printed Part G`, `Printed Part H`, and `Printed Part I` as shown.

## Four-Side Printed Parts Assembly

- Combine the front frame, left and right side frames, and rear frame into a complete outer frame.
- Align the four corner connection points, then fasten them with `CM3*6 self-tapping screws`.
- After assembly, check that the outer frame is flat and that all four corners fit properly.

    src={require('./img/panda-vent-install-step7.webp').default}

## Install the Vent Kit

- Place the vent kit into the corresponding mounting position inside the frame.
- Secure the vent kit and related connectors in the direction shown.
- Make sure the vent opens and closes smoothly without interfering with the housing or cables.

<ImageView
    src={require('./img/panda-vent-install-step8.webp').default}
    width="70%"
/>

## Assemble the PCB and Cable Decorative Cover

- Install the PCB in the specified position inside the frame.
- Secure the PCB with `CM3*6 self-tapping screws`.
- Organize the cables into the corresponding routing grooves, then install the cable cover plate.
- After assembly, check that no cables are pinched and that all connectors are firmly attached.

<ImageView
    src={require('./img/panda-vent-install-step9.webp').default}
    width="70%"
/>

## Install Panda Vent onto the Printer Top

- Remove the glass top cover from the printer.
- Align Panda Vent with the top of the printer, then place it onto the printer from above.
- Place the glass top cover back onto Panda Vent, then check that Panda Vent sits flat and stable.

<ImageView
    src={require('./img/panda-vent-install-step10.webp').default}
    width="70%"
/>

## Installation Check

- All `CM3*6 self-tapping screws` are tightened, with none missing or loose.
- The four-sided frame is assembled evenly, with no obvious gaps or offsets at the joints.
- The illuminated button presses smoothly without sticking.
- The printed lamp shade, printed cover plate, vent kit, and PCB are all installed in place.
- Cables are routed neatly and are not pinched by the housing, cover plated, or vent kit.
- After the assembly is placed on top of the printer, it remains stable and does not interfere with the printer lid or other mechanical movement.

:::tip

After completing the hardware installation, refer to the **Panda Vent Firmware** section to complete the initial setup, wiFi configuration, and printer binding.

:::
