## Product Introduction

The Panda Den Air is a heavy-duty steel workstation designed for the Bambu Lab A/P/X series. As the foundation of the BIQU Panda-Verse, it integrates a waste collection drawer and tool storage directly beneath your machine to reclaim valuable desk space.

Optional RGB lighting adds personalized style and real-time status feedback, while printable add-ons—including a build plate rack and filament spool holder - allow you to further streamline and organize your essential printing workspace.

## Product Features

- **Desk-Space Reclamation**: Positioned directly beneath your Bambu Lab printer, this heavy-duty steel workstation maximizes efficiency without taking up extra desk space.

- **Integrated Storage & Modularity**: Features a high-capacity waste collection drawer and a dedicated compartment for essential tools like scrapers, pliers, and liquid glue. This modular foundation supports functional printable add-ons, specifically a build plate rack and filament spool holder.

- **Optional Lighting Kit**: Provides personalized style and real-time printer status feedback. Lighting effects can be easily managed via a wireless Web UI.

- **Industrial-Grade Build**: Constructed from high-strength, seamless steel with integrated soft-close dampers for a smooth, premium drawer experience and lasting durability.

## Installation Guide

### Bambu Lab P/X Series

- Install the Filament Spool Holder Bracket.

    Retrieve the Filament Spool Holder Bracket Hardware from the drawer. Use the two included **M4x5 button head screws** to secure it to the mounting holes at the rear of the main body as shown.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/panda-series/module/panda-den-air/img/panda-den-air-12.webp').default} width="70%"/>

- Install the spool holder.

    Secure the spool holder included with the Bambu Lab P series onto the bracket as shown.

    P1 Series Reference

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/panda-series/module/panda-den-air/img/panda-den-air-13.webp').default} width="70%"/>

    Since the Panda Den Air is designed to be compatible with Bambu Lab P/X/A series machines, there are two openings on the top: one for the A-series waste outlet and one for the P/X-series waste outlet. When using P/X series machines, you can use a printed part to seal the A-series waste outlet.
