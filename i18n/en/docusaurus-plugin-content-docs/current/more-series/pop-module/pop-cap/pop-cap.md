## Product Overview

PopCap transforms your Snapmaker U1 into a fully enclosed printing environment — without the bulk of a traditional enclosure. Crafted from high-grade polycarbonate (PC), PopCap delivers exceptional heat resistance and crystal-clear visibility, letting you print demanding materials like ABS and nylon with greater consistency — while keeping your workspace noticeably quieter. When it's time to check on your print or swap out tools, the magnetic quick-release hatch opens effortlessly. A built-in top storage slot keeps your essentials organized and always within reach. Sleek, functional, and ready for your next mod — PopCap is the ultimate companion for your Snapmaker U1.

## Product Features

- **Transparent, Heat-Resistant Material**: PopCap is made from one-piece molded polycarbonate (PC), providing excellent impact resistance, heat resistance up to 120$^\circ\mathrm{C}$, and a clear view of the printing process.

- **One-Piece Design for Easy Installation**: Thanks to its one-piece construction, PopCap can be placed directly on top of the Snapmaker U1, making installation quick and effortless.

- **DIY-Friendly Expandability**: PopCap includes several expansion-friendly features, such as a top storage slot for commonly used tools like cutters, scrapers, etc. It also includes reserved cable-routing holes at the rear for Panda Breath and PopStatus, as well as mounting holes at the front for PopTouch.

- **Magnetic Access Hatch**: The access hatch features a magnetic attachment for quick and easy opening and closing. It provides convenient access for removing prints and performing routine maintenance.

## Installation Guide for Snapmaker U1

- Step 1: Identify the correct orientation of the hatch: Place the hatch over the PopCap opening and check which side aligns with the magnetic attachment. Once confirmed, remove it again to make installation easier. Marking the correct side in advance is recommended.

- Step 2: Install the handle: Use the included M4 x 6 screws and Phillips screwdriver to attach the handle to the hatch, as shown in the illustration. Then place the hatch onto the PopCap opening.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-4.webp').default} width="70%"/>

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-5.webp').default} width="70%"/>

- Step 3: Position PopCap directly on top of the Snapmaker U1. Installation is now complete.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-6.webp').default} width="70%"/>

## Recommended Sealing Method

The product includes 0.5 mm single-sided EVA foam tape. It helps secure PopCap more firmly on top of Snapmaker U1, greatly improves the sealing performance, and can also slightly reduce the noise generated during Snapmaker U1 printing.

:::tip

Please note that once the foam tape is applied, it is difficult to remove cleanly. Also, after repeatedly placing and removing PopCap with the foam tape attached, some foam may remain on the side edges of the Snapmaker U1 body. If this is not acceptable, you can choose not to apply the included foam tape. PopCap can still be used normally.

:::

- Step 1: Take out the included EVA foam tape and the PopCap.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-8.webp').default} width="45%"/>

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-9.webp').default} width="45%"/>

- Step 2: Apply the foam tape along the inner edge of the lower opening of the PopCap. Keep it as flat and smooth as possible, and avoid the cable routing hole.

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-11.webp').default} width="45%"/>

    <ImageView src={require('@site/docs/more-series/pop-module/pop-cap/img/pop-popcap-12.webp').default} width="45%"/>

- Step 3: Place PopCap onto the top of Snapmaker U1, then press it down firmly.
